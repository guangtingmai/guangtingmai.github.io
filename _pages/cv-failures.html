Academic setbacks after leaving my hometown started from three consecutive years of failed (not-even-anywhere-close) MPhil/PhD applications in Hong Kong followed by an MSc application to Canada in the fourth year due to substandard undergrad performances, and then to the UK (possibly because of an ulta-late submission in March/April).

By the end of PhD, I failed at four project grant applications (all rejected at the first round) that tried to secure support for a postdoc position. Meanwhile I failed at four interviews in a row before starting the first postdoc at Nottingham during the midst of the Pandemic. I failed at a faulty postion application a year later (sent out without feedback).

I failed at five fellowship applications - three before/during the Pandemic (Sir Henry Wellcome, British Academy, NIHR); two afterwards (Wellcome ECA, MRC Career Development).

Keep Calm and Carry On!
