I am a Wellcome Research Fellow at the MRC Cognition and Brain Sciences Unit (CBU) at Cambridge.

I train as a cognitive neuroscientist studying human brain processing of speech and language. My Wellcome fellowship studies speech processing by hearing-impaired adults with cochlear implants, specifically, how their brains' different sensory (auditory and visual) systems work together to integrate audiovisual information and support speech comprehension (CrossModal-CI). I am also parts of ongoing projects studying neural processing of speech by adults sufferring from aphasia after a stroke and studying neural processes during social interactions in typically hearing or cochlear implanted children.
