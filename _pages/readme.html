I am a Wellcome Research Fellow at the MRC Cognition and Brain Sciences Unit (CBU) at Cambridge.

I train as a cognitive neuroscientist studying human brain processing of speech and language. My Wellcome fellowship studies neural processes that underlie how the brain integrates audio and visual information during speech comprehension by adult cochlear implant users (CrossModal-CI). I am also involved in ongoing projects studying neural processing of speech comphrehension by post-stroke aphasic adults and studying neural processes during social interactions in typical hearing or cochlear implanted children.
