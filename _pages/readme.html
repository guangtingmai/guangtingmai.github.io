I am a Wellcome Research Fellow at the MRC Cognition and Brain Sciences Unit (CBU) at Cambridge.

I train as a cognitive neuroscientist studying human brain processing of speech and language. My Wellcome fellowship studies speech processing in hearing-impaired adults with cochlear implants, specifically, how different sensory (auditory and visual) systems work together in these individuals' brains to integrate audiovisual speech information and support their speech comprehension (CrossModal-CI). I am also parts of ongoing studies into neural speech processing in adults sufferring from post-stroke aphasia and neural processes during social interactions in typically hearing or cochlear implanted children.
